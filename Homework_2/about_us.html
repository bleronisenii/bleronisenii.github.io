- Barça Academy is the official academy of FC Barcelona, dedicated to training young athletes through football while fostering personal development. With a unique educational model based on the Barça methodology, we aim to help students grow as both sportspeople and individuals.

- Our programs cater to boys and girls aged 6 to 18, replicating the “Barça style” game model established by FC Barcelona’s coaching teams. The academy promotes values such as respect, teamwork, humility, effort, and ambition, while expanding the Barça brand globally.

- Each year, tens of thousands of young players participate in activities organized by Barça Academy across its 30 locations in 34 countries. Our programs include:

  • Barça Escola Barcelona:

    - A local football training school for children aged 6 to 12, focusing on fundamental skills and Barça values.

    - Discover the 3 types of sport you can play in BARCELONA:

    1. Football
    2. Basketball
    3. Indoor Football
  • Barça Escola Development Catalunya:

    - Offering additional training across Catalonia for players aged 6 to 14 who seek to enhance their skills.

  • Barça Academy:

    - International academies that provide comprehensive training based on the Barça football model, promoting the club's work ethic worldwide.

  • Barça Academy PRO:

    - Strategic international academies located in key markets, including New York, Miami, and Brazil.

    - Currently, FC Barcelona has 2 Barça Academies PRO, located in New York and Miami.

    1. BARÇA ACADEMY PRO MIAMI
    2. BARÇA ACADEMY PRO NEW YORK
  • Barça Residency Academy:

    - The leading residential sports academy in the United States, located in Casa Grande, Arizona, focusing on the holistic development of young soccer players.
